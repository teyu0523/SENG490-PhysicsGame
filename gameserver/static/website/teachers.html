For Teachers

Let your students experience lessons. Give them a chance to play.

Teach students via simulation

Sometimes concepts are just hard to teach. Some students need to try the egg dropping project, or create rube goldberg machines before a concept will fall into place. InuCo takes this idea and makes it available for every topic! Teach your students concepts of kinetics, elastic collision, springs and more using flexible simulations that are completely under your control. Add and remove variables at will, constrain values according to your student's level of understanding. InuCo supports lesson creation, tests and quizes as well as homework assignments, and can easily be run inside the classroom.

Take control of homework

Incuo finally gives you a window into your students homework habits. See which students complete assignments and when, and look directly at their answers all online, and all as the students are working. Adjust answers, reset data and take control of the homework process. You no longer have to rely on parents at home to monitor the student.

Marking made easy

Incuo automatically marks all assignments and questions. This means no more late nights marking students homework. That being said, a computer doesn't always account for effort or partial work, so marking is flexible and completely under your control. Dole out partial marks at will!
