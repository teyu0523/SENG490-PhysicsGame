Introducing InuCo the ultimate
learning game framework.

InuCo is complete learning game framework designed to help teachers provide interactive experiences to their students that help reinforce the lessons learned in class.

Real Time Applications

InuCo teaches students via gamified highschool questions. Students get to use real-time worlds and systems play around with the concepts taught in class.

Learning Made Fun

By replacing typical explainations and assignment formats with ones that are more comparable to the games students play at home InuCo brings a sense of fun to the classroom.

Cloud Storage

All data is stored on the cloud. No worrying about databases going down. No worrying about losing lessons, or student information.

Mobile Support

InuCo runs on a large vareity of devices and operations systems, including but not limited to Android, IOS, and Windows Phone.

For Students

InuCo provides a new learning environment for students. By running the application on their phones, tablets, or PCs students unlock a world of real time feedback and intrinsic learning typically not available in the classroom.
