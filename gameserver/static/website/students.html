Students

Play your way through class problems, learn while having fun!

Every question is a game

Inuco's goal is to make your assignments fun. By playing games on your ipad, home computer, or even your phone, you can complete all of your homework without even touching a pen and paper. Inuco also helps with visuallizing the concepts your teachers give you in class. Watch two objects crash into eachother, fire tank rounds that arc into your target. Use a spring to launch your player into the air. All of these demonstrations are available. You can watch the values taught in class change as you simulation runs, and really develop a feel for what's happening. Even written assignments can be done in inuco. Solve math problems, write short answer questions, all while on the bus home from school. Even in class tests can be done with visual represenation. You'll never have to ask your teacher to clarify a question again!

Every device is a portal

Use your phone, tablet, laptop, or even a school computer to play Inuco. All of your assigmnents and classes are available on each one. Use touch controls, a keyboard or even a controller while doing your homework. It's flexibility at its best!

Every class is just better

Inuco brings your school into the 21st century. You can finally use the technology you love to help improve your learning. By making everything easy for teachers and students Inuco makes those last steps towards a modern classroom easy.
